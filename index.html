"Educationg the leaders of tomorrow who will change how the world lives,learns,and works"





In the year of 2010, Queen Mira International School was started with the mission to implement CBSE syllabus among the learning community to originate an scholarly ambience that enhances and develops the mind of a child with an advancement in all the spheres instead of just focusing on academics.

We live in an interconnected and complex world that is changing at an unheard of pace. Preparing our children to succeed in this challenging environment demands a flexible and real world based approach to education. QMIS maneuvers to reach this widened horizon through its Philosophy, Vision, Mission and Core Values.

“We at QMIS believe in ensuring the well being of the body, mind and soul of the individual by instilling a steady growth mindset, who learn and unlearn to emerge as change makers.”The global vision of QMIS is realized through its mission of Any where any time learning environment Collaborative learning environment of students and teachers provide opportunities to develop personnel and professional attributes Deliver value based education that produces responsible global citizens who act as a change factor in the society.
